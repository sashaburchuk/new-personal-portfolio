About

A furniture and concrete fabricator by trade, I also specialize in product design, interior design and coordinating construction projects.

My work draws on Debord's theory of psychogeography and focuses on influencing human habits and behavioral patterns through the built environment. I work with space, light, form and material to create simple and functional furniture and habitats.
