Rancho Relaxo Cabin

Rancho Relaxo is an A-frame in Rhododendron, Oregon on the flanks of Mt. Hood. We gutted it and redesigned it to be a modern and minimal retreat, with user experience research as our guiding principal. Currently a vacation rental on AirBnB and hopefully in the near future, home to an artist residency.
